WordPress

WordPress is an extremely scalable platform for any business. It can be used for blogging, a simple website, an eCommerce store and everything in between! Don't have much experience building a website? No problem! It's extremely user friendly with a strong community for technincal support. There are tons of plugins that can be installed to make your site totally customized to fit your needs. Not only does having a website help establish your business' credibility but allows you to market your business.

Open Web Analytics

This easy to install, open source web analytics software allows you to track and analyze how people use your website and applications. Want to track where your website traffic is coming from? Gauge the success of marketing actitives? Then OWA (Open Web Analytics) is perfect for you!

  • Go to App

Matomo

Matomo aims to be an open source alternative to Google Analytics. It provides detailed reports on not only your website and its visitors but keywords searched, which pages visitors like, where you are losing customers and much more. With the over 30+ list of reports that can be pulled through Matomo, the data you need to make your business a success is right at your finger tips!
